Two Slit Photon: Examples


To view an example, press the button at the end of the description. Once the module finishes loading, press the play button at the recording panel. If you want to save the example code to your computer and run it locally right click of the disk icon and save the file to disk. Use wsl as an extension of the file you save to your computer.

Example 1  400 nm light, 10,000 photons
In this example light of wavelength 400 nm is normally incident upon two slits. The width of each slit is 0.04 mm, and the distance between the slits is 0.25 mm. A total of 10,000 photons arrive at the observation screen.

Example 2  700 nm Light, 10,000 Photons
In this example light of wavelength 700 nm is normally incident upon two slits. The width of each slit is 0.04 mm, and the distance between the slits is 0.25 mm. A total of 10,000 photons arrive at the observation screen.
