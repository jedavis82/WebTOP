Two Slit Photon: Exercises


Exercise 1

Starting from the default configuration, click the Audio On button to activate the audio feature of this module. Click the Play button to start the simulation. Without clearing the observation screen, click the Play button a second time to send in more photons.

Exercise 2

Starting from the default configuration, change the number of photons/sec to 200, and the exposure time to 25 seconds. Click the Play button to start the simulation.
