Photoelectric Effect: Images

Definitions
λ = wavelength in vacuum of light incident upon Plate 1 (plate on left)
I = intensity of light incident upon Plate 1
Work function = energy required to remove an electron from Plate 1
V = potential difference across plates (Plate 1 is at higher voltage)


Image 1
λ = 400 mm, I = 2.0 (arbitrary units), Work function = 2.50 eV, V = 0 volts.
